What is this thing?

The goal of the game is to gain as many points as possible by guessing Underground, Overground and DLR station locations around London.

This is a hobby project to play around with web technologies.

Who built it?

It is built by Karl Kerem over couple of week-ends.

Is it open-source?

Yes, have a blast, fork GitHub repo and do whatever you want to it.

Where is the map taken from?

Original tube map was built in CSS (by hand!) by John Galantini to celebrate 150 years of London Underground. (GitHub Repo).

I salute his effort and encourage you to check out John's personal page.
